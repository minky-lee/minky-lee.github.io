Resi

2019 - 2020 / UX / UI

Resi is the first online residential architects in the UK, helping homeowners all over the country from the very first steps of their projects to the end. Using the admin system and customer dashboard that's built by Resi's in-house tech team with screen share software and scanning devices, customers can easily share everything important for their project from inspirations to architectural drawings without hassle, no matter where they are.
