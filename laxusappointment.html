Laxus X -
appointment
system

2018 / UX / UI

Laxus is a Japanese service that lends luxurious handbags to membership holders with low price. They also borrow those bags from their users who own those bags but don't really use them, repair them, lend them to other users and pay the owner.

For the people who want to lend their bag to Laxus, they have an appointment system. But the design was not mobile-friendly and not organised so the company decided to redesign the process.
